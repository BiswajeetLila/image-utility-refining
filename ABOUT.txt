IMAGE UTILITY
=============

A lightweight, fast desktop tool for quick image batch tasks.

WHY IT EXISTS
Built to avoid heavy tools like Photoshop (which eat RAM/VRAM).
Focused on the repetitive tasks your art team does daily:
removing backgrounds, splitting spritesheets, stitching atlases.

No bloat. No subscription. Runs offline. Works on any PC.


WHAT IT DOES
- Background Removal: Strip backgrounds from single images or
  entire folders in seconds using AI.
- Grid Split: Cut images into grid cells (spritesheets, tiles).
- Atlas Creator: Stitch numbered images into a single spritesheet.
- Real-time Logs: See exactly what the app is doing.

All models bundled. Zero downloads ever needed.


FUTURE
Built to grow. More artist workflows coming as the team needs them.
Designed to be hackable — feedback drives new features.
